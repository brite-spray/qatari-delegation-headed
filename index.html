He did it again, Qatari Minister of Finance Ali Shareef Al Emadi participated in the annual autumn conference

November 1, 2019 Justin Irvin 0 Comments

Last October 14 to 20 Minister of Finance Ali Shareef Al Emadi had the chance to met the Prime Minister of the Federal Republic of Somalia. During these days Qatar took part in the annual autumn meetings of the IMF and the World Bank Group. The event was held in Washington, DC. 

Aside from the Prime Minister, Al Emadi also met with Steven Mnuchin, U.S. Secretary of the Treasury, Sudanese Minister of Finance, Dr. Ibrahim Al Badawi, and a host of ministers involved in the meetings and heads of bank boards.

The Qatari delegation, led by Ali Shareef Al Emadi, held various meetings with senior officials from the IMF and the World Bank Group, along with bilateral meetings with delegations from other nations.

During the meeting, the Committee discussed ways to further improve economic and financial coordination among members of the GCC and boost revenue. The autumn conference brings together thousands of government officials, the private sector and experts from around the world to discuss issues of global concern such as the global economic outlook, eradication of poverty, economic development and aid efficiency. Regional conferences, seminars and many other activities and events focusing on the global economy, international development, and global financial markets will also happen throughout these conferences.

The accompanying delegation participated in a number of consultative meetings to discuss financial policies, recent developments and the economic difficulties facing the world at the regional and international levels.
